What it manages

The system keeps accepted-offer work organized across brokerage staff, agents, attorney offices, lender offices, inspectors, appraisers, title contacts, buyers, and sellers.

Deal Sheet Intake

Capture property, price, deposit, mortgage, attorneys, agents, additional terms, property condition status, and acknowledgments.

Transaction Tracker

Track what is missing, who is responsible, who was contacted, due dates, blockers, and status.

Communication Log

Record calls, emails, texts, confirmations, follow-ups, office contacts, paralegals, processors, inspectors, and appraisers.

Offer Review

Review the file for missing facts, discrepancies, open blockers, contract-readiness, and attorney handoff readiness.

Contacts

Reuse attorneys, agents, lenders, inspectors, appraisers, title contacts, coordinators, and office staff.

Printable and Email-Ready

Print a clean deal sheet and generate start-email drafts inside the deal file for copy/paste sending.
